About Me

My name is Karen Lopez, a Copenhagen-based architect with a passion for creating spaces that resonate with the human spirit. My journey began with a fascination for how light interacts with raw materials, leading me to pursue a Master's degree at the Royal Danish Academy.

Over the past decade, I've worked across residential, cultural, and urban scales, always seeking the "softness" in architecture. I believe that a building should not just stand, but belong—to its landscape, its history, and its people.
