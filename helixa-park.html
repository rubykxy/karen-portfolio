Public Plaza Exploration

Helixa Park

An exploration of public landscape integration

Context & Aerial Perspective

A plaza that moves with its visitors. Helixa Park is inspired by the organic courtyards of Tokyo, Japan, using curving pathways and layered spaces to create movement and encourage interaction.

The flowing layout promotes balance, inviting people to explore, linger, and engage with both the environment and each other. Subtle shifts in elevation and spatial hierarchy provide moments of pause and reflection throughout the park.

The plaza unfolds across three experiential levels, highlighting art, gathering, and contemplation, while placing accessibility at the center of the design to ensure that all visitors can enjoy the space.
